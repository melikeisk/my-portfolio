Tin Dog

Google 1998 Beta

Have you ever seen Google's 1998 beta version? If you haven't, then it means you haven't experienced the simple yet effective state of the internet at that time. But don't worry, I've recreated it exactly for you. This beta version sheds light on the period when Google was still a young and evolving technology as a search engine. The web page had a minimalist design, and unlike today's complex search algorithms, the search results were quite basic and straightforward. However, since then, Google has continuously improved the search experience and added innovations to make users' lives easier. Google's 1998 beta version offers an intriguing flashback when considering how far we've come, serving as a significant indicator of how the internet has evolved.
